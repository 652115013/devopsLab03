Chiang Mai University (CMU) has recently inaugurated the College of Arts, Media, and Technology (CAMT), a significant development in northern Thailand. CAMT is dedicated to advancing human resource development and fostering innovation in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) within the tourism, handicraft, and software industries in Northern Thailand. Key points about CAMT: Mission and Focus: CAMT aims to contribute to the development of the tourism, handicraft, and software sectors in Northern Thailand. The college focuses on human resource development and innovation in ICT. Academic Programs: CAMT is set to offer undergraduate programs in critical areas such as software engineering, animation, e-tourism, and handicraft design. Post-graduate Opportunities: CAMT provides opportunities for post-graduate education, offering Master's and Doctoral degrees to experienced knowledge workers. Research Initiatives: Research groups within CAMT are established to support industrial research, emphasizing practical applications and advancements in ICT. Overall, CAMT represents a strategic initiative by CMU to foster education, innovation, and research in key technology-driven sectors, aligning with the region's economic and industrial priorities.
